During Alex's stay in the Pediatric ICU in Duke University Medical Center, we found a Parent's Prayer which had been donated on a plaque by another family who had a simliar journey with their child. We wanted to share this with you.

Read the Parents Prayer

In leu of flowers, contributions should be made to:

Alex Eash Memorial Fund
c/o Raleigh Mennonite Church
PO Box 11282
Raleigh, NC 27604

We set up this fund to collect monies to then be used to purchase items to be donated to the Pediatric Intensive Care Unit and the Pediatric Ward (5300) of Duke University Medical Center. During our stay, there were many items which were donated by other families that helped us, helped the nursing staff, or helped Alex. We want to return that favor, and do what we can for the other families who are and will be going through similar journeys with their child.
